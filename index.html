A note

Ibuild production software for the markets I actually live in. That means thinking in CFA francs, mobile money rails, and Francophone interfaces from the start — not retrofitting Western templates after the fact.

The work spans fintech, edtech, ride-hailing, logistics — anywhere there's a real gap between what local users need and what's actually shipping. I tend to ship the whole thing: backend, frontend, native mobile, infrastructure. Generalist by necessity, specialist by interest.
