Purpose

The main purpose of the website will be to inform users about the weather, their benefits, and implementation best practices. This objective will be fulfilled through the descriptions of what an WhatWeather is and what isn't, explanation of design principles and the main tenets of the WhatWeather paradigm, as well as through a close coaching to with disabilities .

A secondary purpose is to provide a source of coaching and advice on outfit equipements to wear according the weather implementation and design patterns for safety. This will be presented in the form of lists of patterns, withdrawn from various sources, and links to authoritative sources of services.
