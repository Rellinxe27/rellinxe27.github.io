Cancellation Information

You may cancel your reservation up to 30 days in advance. Cancelling within 30 days of the scheduled departure requires paying a cancellation fee that may be used as credit towards a future reservation. To cancel your reservation, please contact ~Scoots at 1-700-281-4590. Our office is open Monday-Saturday 8:00AM to 4:00PM MDT.

If there are documented extenuating circumstances, we will waive the cancellation fee. In addition, if you scheduled a reservation between July 17th and October 25th, you can adjust your trip dates without a change fee for up to six months from the date you reserved.
